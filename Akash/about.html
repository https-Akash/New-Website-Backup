My Story

My Journey

Hello! I'm Akash Mishra, a commerce student who discovered a passion for web development. What started as a hobby during a particularly boring summer vacation has evolved into a serious pursuit alongside my academic studies.

Growing up in Deoria, Uttar Pradesh, I was always drawn to computers and technology. My first interaction with code was through simple HTML tutorials I found online, and I was immediately hooked by the ability to create something from nothing.

The Unusual Blend

As a commerce student, my academic path doesn't typically align with technical fields like web development. However, I believe this unique combination gives me a different perspective. I can blend business acumen with technical skills, understanding both the functional and technical aspects of digital projects.

When I'm not studying or coding, you'll find me immersed in video games. Gaming has been a constant companion throughout my life, providing not just entertainment but also inspiration for my design work and problem-solving approach.
